Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about getting started and working together.

How quickly will you respond to my message?

I typically respond to all messages within 24 hours, often much sooner. For urgent matters, you can also reach out via phone or schedule a call directly.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes! The 30-minute discovery call is completely free with no obligations. It's an opportunity for us to get to know each other and see if we're a good fit to work together.

What information should I include in my message?

The more details you provide about your goals, current fitness level, challenges, and timeline, the better I can tailor my response to your specific needs.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Absolutely! I offer both in-person and virtual consultations via video call. This allows me to work with clients regardless of their location.

What happens after I send my message?

I'll review your message and respond with personalized recommendations and next steps. If it seems like a good fit, I'll invite you to schedule a free consultation call.
