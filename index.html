About Me

Meet Sabrina

Sabrina Fan is a certified health & supplement consultant, functional trainer, and inspiring speaker who is the CEO of Iron Butterfly Coaching. With expertise in holistic wellness and functional movement, she empowers individuals to achieve optimal health through personalized training, strategic supplementation, and lifestyle optimization. Her mission is to help you build sustainable habits that transform not just your body, but your entire approach to wellness.
